FOUNDING PROVISIONS AND POLITICAL PRINCIPLES

ColouredCodez is a North West University programming club that will partake in most activities involving software development in the university. We stand together with all students majoring or with an interest in computer science, in particular programming and with all students who share the common goal of promoting, advancing and improving computer science in the university.

PRINCIPLES

The vision of the Democratic Alliance is grounded on the defence, promotion and extension of the following principles: The rights and freedoms of every person encompassing the right to freedom of speech and association. Regular elections that are free and fair. A Representative and accountable leadership committee elected democratically. The rejection of any form of discrimination on any grounds.

MISSION STATEMENT

As a recognized society of the university I commit myself to serve with integrity, devotion and a deep desire to improve the learning experience and skills of all computer science students.

PROGRAMME OF ACTION

The club will promote and extend its reach within the computer science department and outside it. The programme of action must include provisions aiming at, but not be limited to, the following: Serving and catering to the needs of all students facing challenges in the computer science department. Being an effective student society when active. Holding its public representatives to account.
